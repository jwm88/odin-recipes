Steamed Rice

Description

You probably suck at cooking rice. Here's how to do it.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water

Method

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Put the rice in a pot and shake it a little so it sits level
  3. Put your finger in the rice and use your thumb to measure how deep it is
  4. Using your finger and thumb, put the tip of you finger ontop of the rice and fill with water up to your thumb
  5. Cover and cook on high heat until it begins to bubble up
  6. Turn to minimum heat once the rice is bubbling
  7. Cook over minimum heat for 15 minutes
  8. Turn off the heat and let the rice absorb the water for another 15 minutes
  9. Fluff the rice and let stand with the lid off for 5 minutes
