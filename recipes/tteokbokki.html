Tteokbokki

Description

A korean favourite with rich gochujang sauce, sesame seeds, and toppings like tofu and mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • Gochujang (korean spice paste)
  • Gochujaru (korean chilli flakes)
  • Tteok (rice cakes, sliced or tubular)
  • Aburaage (tofu)
  • Mushroom Powder
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 Carrot
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Black Vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Any additional toppings such as green onions, tofu, mushrooms, sesame seeds etc

Method

  1. Fry 2 sliced onions,1 thinkly sliced carrot, and lots minced garlic in a large pot
  2. Boil 4 cups of water and add to the pot
  3. Add 1 T each of mushroom powder, nutritional yeast, vegan stock powder, soy sauce, and black vinegar
  4. Add 1/4 cup of gochujang
  5. Add 1 t gochujaru (more = hotter)
  6. Allow to simmer for some time
  7. Add Tteok and Aburaage, simmer until the tteok are soft
  8. Garnish with green onion and sesame seeds and serve.
